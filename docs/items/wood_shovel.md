# wood shovel

The wood shovel is a basic tool used for digging soil, sand, gravel, and other similar materials.

## Appearance

![wood shovel icon](/img/items/wood_shovel.png)

## Details

* **Type:** Tool
* **Stack size:** 1
* **placeable:** No

## Usage

The wood shovel can be used to dig and collect soil, sand, gravel, and other similar materials.

## Crafting

Crafting a wood shovel requires a [crafting table](/docs/items/craft_table).

To craft a wood shovel, you will need the following materials:
* 5 [wood](/docs/items/wood)

You will have to do the following steps:
* [sculpting](/docs/crafting/sculpting)

One craft will give you 1 wood shovel.
