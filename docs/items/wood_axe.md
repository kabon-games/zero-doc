# wood axe

The wood axe is a basic tool used for chopping wood and wooden objects.

## Appearance

![wood axe icon](/img/items/wood_axe.png)

## Details

* **Type:** Tool
* **Stack size:** 1
* **placeable:** No

## Usage

The wood axe can be used to chop down trees and wooden blocks.

## Crafting

Crafting a wood axe requires a [crafting table](/docs/items/craft_table).

To craft a wood axe, you will need the following materials:
* 5 [wood](/docs/items/wood)

You will have to do the following steps:
* [sculpting](/docs/crafting/sculpting)

One craft will give you 1 wood axe.
