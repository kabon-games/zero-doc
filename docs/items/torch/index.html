torch

The torch is a light source item used to illuminate dark areas.

Appearance​

Details​

  • Type: Light source
  • Stack size: 200
  • placeable: Yes

Usage​

The torch can be placed on the ground or walls to provide light in dark areas.

Crafting​

Crafting a torch does not require any special crafting station.

To craft a torch, you will need the following materials:

  • 3 wood

There are no special crafting steps required.

One craft will give you 5 torches.

Block​

Appearance​

Details​

  • Break level: Hands
  • Resistance: 0.5
  • Background: No
  • Passthrough: Yes

Usage​

The torch provides a light of 5 units radius when placed.

Versions​

  • Added in version 1.0
